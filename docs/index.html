FAQs

Got Questions?

How many guests can each package serve?
Packages are designed to serve up to the stated guest count with one standard serving per guest.
Can I customize the toppings?
Yes! Additional toppings and premium upgrades are available as add-ons to any package.
Do you travel outside Quezon City?
Yes. Out-of-town fees may apply depending on location. Contact us for a custom quotation.
How long is the service?
Standard service window is up to 2 hours. Additional hours can be arranged for an extra fee.
What happens if more guests arrive?
Additional servings may be arranged and charged accordingly, subject to availability.
Is the reservation fee refundable?
Reservation fees are non-refundable but may be transferable to another date based on approval.
