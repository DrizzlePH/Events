Got Questions?

Frequently Asked

Everything you need to know before booking your Drizzle Cart.

How many guests can each package serve?+
Each package is designed to serve up to the stated guest count with one standard serving per guest. Our Drop Cart handles up to 50 guests, Drizzle Cart up to 100, Downpour Cart up to 150, and Thunderstorm Cart up to 200.
Can I customize the toppings?+
Yes! You can choose from our full toppings menu when booking your package. Additional toppings and premium upgrades (like Nutella, Biscoff, or Pistachio sauce) are available as add-ons.
Do you travel outside your city?+
Yes, we do! Out-of-town events are welcome — an out-of-town fee will apply depending on the distance and location. Contact us for a custom quotation.
How long is the service?+
Our standard service window is up to 2 hours. If you need more time, you can add extra service hours at ₱1,500 per hour.
What happens if more guests arrive than expected?+
Additional servings can be arranged and charged accordingly. You can also pre-purchase additional guest slots as add-ons — 25 extra guests at ₱2,500, or 50 extra guests at ₱4,500.
What is your booking and payment policy?+
Guest counts must be finalized before the event. Reservation fees are non-refundable but may be transferable subject to approval. Venue access, permits, and parking are the client's responsibility unless otherwise agreed.
